## 3. Major Challenges and Opportunities
### Challenges:
- **Revenue Decline:** A noted decline in iPhone and Mac sales attributes to customer perception of incremental updates and growing competition.
- **Regulatory Scrutiny:** Apple faces increasing investigations regarding potential monopolistic practices, particularly related to App Store policies.
- **Supply Chain Issues:** Global supply chain disruptions have affected production capabilities, leading to further revenue challenges.

### Opportunities:
- **Expansion of Services:** With the Services division projected to contribute 24% of Apple's total revenue in 2024, enhancements in software and subscription services present substantial growth opportunities.
- **New Product Categories:** The anticipated launch of innovative products, such as the Apple Vision Pro and revamped iPhone 15, is expected to rejuvenate consumer interest and drive sales.
